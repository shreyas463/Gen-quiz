Welcome to the Quiz Generator


The quiz generator allows you to upload a pdf, on which questions will be generated on each page.

It can be of 3 types - Fill in the blank questions, Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ's) or "Wh" type questions.

You will have to give your answer in a multiple choice format, i.e, you have to select one of the 4 options given to answer your question.
